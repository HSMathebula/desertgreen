About Us

Problems we are solving

  • Low levels of education among farmer
  • Lack of technical & scientific knowledge from Extension Officers
  • High Extension Officer – Farmer Ratio
  • High costs of Inputs; Machinery & Implements for small-scale farmers
  • Difficulty for small-scale farmers & Informal traders to find one another
  • No cheap delivery & storage system for informal traders
  • High Food waste resulting from so-called “Ugly-produce” rejected from retail & wholesale
  • No credit-worthiness because they are mostly unbanked
  • Not bankable due to productivity & low income growth
  • Unable to attract micro-investments from individuals/stokvels due to lack of traceability

Africa is the world’s last resort for food security

  • Home to over 60% of the world’s remaining arable land.
  • More than 62% jobs in Africa are in Agriculture.
  • Average age of a farmer is 60 years with 65% of the population aged 18-35 years.
  • Over 30-40% of Africa’s trade is informal, as high as 77% in East & West Africa.

Our Soulution

Africa has the opportunity to leverage its youthful population and its increasing mobile broadband penetration to improve productivity in small-scale farming.
