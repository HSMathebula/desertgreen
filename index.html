We are an Agritech start-up founded on the core-mission of tackling food insecurity challenges in the informal agri-value chain in Africa. We build mobile tech solutions to improve productivity, profits & livelihoods for Africa small-scale farmers & Informal traders while bringing youth back to farming.

Co-founded in 2015, formally registered in 2016, by 3 young entrepreneurs from different walks of life with 3 things in common: a rural upbringing, a passion for Agri-tech & a near-impossible vision for a self-sustaining connected Africa.
